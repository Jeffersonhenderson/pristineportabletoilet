Frequently Asked Questions

1. I have a beautifully landscaped lawn. Will a portable toilet cause damage?

We are extremely careful during placement to protect your property. Our units are lightweight and our technicians are trained to place them gently. For sensitive surfaces, we can discuss placement on a driveway or a less conspicuous area. For long-term placements on grass, we recommend a slight shift during service to prevent any lasting impact.

2. What supplies are included with a standard porta potty rental?

Every portable restroom rental in Orchard Hills, PA from Pristine Portable Toilet Rental comes fully stocked and ready to use. This includes ample toilet paper and a hand sanitizer dispenser. Our deluxe units with sinks also come stocked with soap and paper towels.

3. How much clearance does your delivery truck need to place a unit?

Our service trucks require a clear, stable path to the desired placement location. Generally, we need a path with a minimum width of about 10-12 feet and an overhead clearance of at least 14 feet, free from low-hanging branches or wires.

4. What is the main difference in the porta potty rental cost between a standard and a deluxe flushing unit?

The cost for a deluxe flushing unit is moderately higher than a standard unit. This price difference reflects the more complex mechanics of the flushing system, the inclusion of a fresh-water sink, and the additional supplies and servicing time required to maintain the water tank and waste system.

5. What should I do if my event has more attendees than I originally planned for?

If you realize your guest count is increasing significantly, please contact us as soon as possible. Depending on the situation and availability, we may be able to add another unit to your order or schedule an additional mid-event service call to ensure your facilities remain clean and functional for the larger crowd.
